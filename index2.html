POORNIMA GROUP AT A GLANCE

Born with a vision of creating an engineering college, Poornima College & Institute was established about 3 decades ago in the year 2000 in Jaipur (Rajasthan).
With the primary aim to offer excellent professional & technical education to the students,
Poornima College & Institute was created under the flagship of Shanti Education Society (SES) that engraved its existence in the year 1999,
thereby registering itself under the Rajasthan Societies act.
